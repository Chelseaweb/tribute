Meet Bonang Matheba

Bonang Dorothy Matheba is a South African television presenter,business woman,producer,model and philanthropist.Born in Mahikeng,North West,she is known for her exquisite presenting skills and her signature voice.She presented the SABC 1 music show LIVE(now live AMP)which built her spotlight in the industry.She is known for being the first black South African to be featured in numerous magazines....

  • Global Brand Ambassandor for Revlon Cosmetic
  • Presenter on SA's Premier Lifestyle and Magazine show
  • First black star to cover Glamour SA December 2014
  • Judge for Miss South Africa Pageant 2014
  • The most style media personality- SA Style Awards
  • Influencer of the year at E People's Choice Of Awards 2019
